Why Global Warming Matters

Global warmming matters because if we just continued living our lives polluting the air with burning plastics at garbage yards, cutting down trees, and farming livestock, the tempature will soon rise because of all the green house gasses in the atmosphere trapping all the heat.

- What makes the green housegases trap heat? -
These gas molecules all are made of three or more atoms. The atoms are held together loosely enough that they vibrate when they absorb heat

Get Involved

You can make a difference in the fight against global warming:

  • Reduce your carbon footprint | Reducing your carbon footprint helps fight global warming by cutting down on harmful gases that make the Earth hotter.

  • Support renewable energy initiatives | Supporting renewable energy helps reduce greenhouse gas emissions and promotes a cleaner, more sustainable future.

  • Advocate for sustainable policies | Advocating for sustainable policies means supporting government actions that protect the environment, conserve resources, and promote fairness.

  • Educate others and raise awareness
